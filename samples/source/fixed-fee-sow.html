Statement of Work

SOW-2026-014 · Data Pipeline Migration · Fixed-Fee Engagement

This Statement of Work (the “SOW”) is entered into as of April 15, 2026 (the “Effective Date”) by and between Phoenix Industries Inc., a Texas corporation (“Client”), and BlueRiver Consulting Group LLC, a California limited liability company (“Provider”), pursuant to the Master Services Agreement between the Parties dated January 10, 2026.

1. Term

This SOW shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue through September 30, 2026, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

2. Scope & Deliverables

Provider shall deliver the following:

  1. Migration of Client’s data pipeline from the legacy Airflow 1.x environment to a managed Dagster deployment, including all DAG conversions and historical-backfill orchestration.
  2. A written runbook documenting the operating procedures, on-call escalation paths, and rollback steps for the new pipeline.
  3. A two-week operational handoff in which Provider’s lead engineer pairs with Client’s on-call team to transfer operational responsibility.

3. Fees & Payment

In consideration for the services described herein, Client shall pay Provider a fixed fee of forty-two thousand U.S. dollars (USD $42,000), payable as follows: $10,000 upon execution of this SOW; $16,000 upon completion of the Dagster cutover; and $16,000 upon completion of the operational handoff. All invoices are due net thirty (30) days from invoice date.

4. Intellectual Property

All work product produced by Provider in performance of this SOW, including the DAGs, runbook, and any code or documentation, shall be considered “work made for hire” and shall be the exclusive property of Client. To the extent any such work product does not qualify as work made for hire, Provider hereby assigns to Client all right, title, and interest therein.

5. Termination

Either Party may terminate this SOW upon fourteen (14) days’ written notice to the other. In the event of termination by Client for convenience, Client shall pay Provider a kill fee equal to twenty-five percent (25%) of any unbilled fees remaining under this SOW, in addition to fees earned through the effective date of termination.

6. Governing Law

This SOW shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

7. Limitation of Liability

Provider’s aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this SOW shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to Provider under this SOW. In no event shall either Party be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages.

Agreed and accepted as of the Effective Date.
